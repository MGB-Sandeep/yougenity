About Yougenity

Yougenity uses personalized genome data codes, called the Geni Code, to connect individuals based on shared genetic characteristics. The company aims to create a community for individuals who have a genuine interest in delving deeper into their personal identities beyond traditional demographic factors. Yougenity provides valuable genetic reports, including health and wellness insights, as well as custom art, music, and character options based on an individual's genome data. The company also plans to offer an AI genetic counselor called Geni-bot, to answer questions and provide guidance on genetic data. Overall, Yougenity is an innovative company that explores the possibilities of personalized genetic data to connect people in new and meaningful ways.
